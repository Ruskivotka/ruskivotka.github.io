About Me

I'm Joni

Mechanical Engineer and Developer

I am a versatile professional with hands-on experience as a vehicle mechanic, truck driver, and engineer. My passion for machinery began at a young age—I have been working with engines since I was 10 years old. I built a custom motorbike showcased at Bike Show 2015 and designed car audio systems that gained national attention on YLE. This enthusiasm for technology and mechanics is complemented by strong skills in project management, 3D design, and VR applications, developed through both formal education and practical experience. Sports have also played a significant role in my life. I have competed in ice hockey and soccer at a high level, including a season in the Finnish Junior Championship league. Additionally, I received the highest grade of 5 for my thesis. I bring the same dedication to all my projects, always striving to be among the best in everything I do.
